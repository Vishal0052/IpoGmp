IPO GMP – Urban Company IPO

About Urban Company

Urban Company (formerly UrbanClap) is India's largest home services platform, connecting millions of customers with skilled service professionals. Founded in 2014, the company offers services including beauty, wellness, home repair, cleaning, and painting across 40+ cities in India, UAE, Australia, and Singapore.

Key Business Highlights:

  • Market leader in home services with 40M+ customers
  • 3M+ service professionals on platform
  • Operating in 40+ cities across 4 countries
  • Strong brand recognition and customer loyalty
  • Technology-driven service delivery model

Investment Rationale

Strengths:

  • Market leadership in growing home services sector
  • Strong technology platform and operational efficiency
  • Diversified service portfolio and geographic presence
  • Improving unit economics and path to profitability
  • Asset-light, scalable business model

Risks:

  • Intense competition from organized and unorganized players
  • Dependency on service partner network
  • Regulatory challenges in multiple geographies
  • Customer acquisition and retention costs
